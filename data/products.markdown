Whole or cut dried roots are used as raw material for powders, extracts, capsules, and traditional Ayurvedic preparations. Ashwagandha roots are valued for their natural adaptogenic properties and are widely used in wellness and nutraceutical applications.
Roots are cleaned, shade-dried, and size-graded to maintain uniformity and quality, and export batches follow moisture-control, hygiene, and botanical verification standards before processing.

**Typical Uses:** Extraction, powder milling, herbal formulations, capsules, tablets, and nutraceutical blends.

| Processing Type | Roots: shade-dried, cleaned and size graded; Powder: mechanically milled & sieved from dried roots |

| Color | Roots: light beige to pale brown; Powder: light cream to yellowish/light brown |

| Product Type | Herbal raw root & nutraceutical powder ingredient |
| Grade Standard | Roots: herbal/medicine grade suitable for extraction; Powder: food/supplement grade |
| Shelf Life | Typically 24+ months from production when stored cool, dry away from light & moisture |
